About

Ranvir in the static

Ranvir is an indie rock / shoegaze project that leans into washed-out guitars, soft vocals, and drum machines left a little too loud. It’s a bedroom origin story that kept spilling onto stages — feedback, late nights, and the kind of choruses that only show up at 3 a.m.

Drawing on everything from Clairo’s diaristic intimacy to Dijon’s live-warm chaos, Ranvir writes songs that sit somewhere between confession and catharsis — small moments that feel bigger under a wall of sound.

The project is based in Sydney, stitching together home-recorded demos, friends’ living rooms, and quick flights to studios when the tapes call for it. Ranvir moves slowly but stays honest: guitars first, algorithms second.

Influences: My Bloody Valentine, Clairo, Dijon, Slowdive, Cocteau Twins.
